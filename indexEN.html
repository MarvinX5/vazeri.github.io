Technological Innovation Fund

National Council for Science and Technology (CONACYT)

I am currently focusing my efforts on the application of the technology readyness methodology of NASA applied by CONACYT, to participate in July 2017 in the call of the Technological Innovation Fund.

The objective is to generate a commercial product, easily assembled and of easy installation that allows real-time monitoring of electrical consumption in residential electrical installations and display the information available in a web application to help people to have an effective management over the cost of the energy used, although there are still details to address within the business model, theres already a significant advance in technology and the methodology.

The development of this project involves knowledge of engineering, computer networks, administration, accounting, business, intellectual property, electronic design and makeup, high and low level programming, as well as research and publication of results in science and technology congresses; I am in the search of partners with the interest of participating in the creation of a product that will lead us to convert it over the next 2 years, into a successful technology-based company.

Research interests

Non Invasive Load Monitoring

Measurement of power in electrical installations without interrupting the flow of electrons in electrical conductors, to provide information to the user about their electricity consumption in real time.

Harmonic Distortions and Energy Quality

How electrical and electronic devices are affected by frequencies that alter fundamental, and how the energy efficiency in installations is altered by this phenomenon.

Energy disagregation

The identification of electronic devices by means of statistical processes through measurement systems.
